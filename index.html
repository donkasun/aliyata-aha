🐘 How to Play

Remember where the elephant's eye is — then place it from memory!

🖱️ Mouse Mode
  1. Press M or click [M] Mouse to start
  2. The elephant appears — memorise the eye position
  3. The elephant hides — move your mouse to where the eye was
  4. Click or press Space to drop the eye
  5. See how close you were!
📷 Camera Mode
  1. Press C or click [C] Camera to start
  2. The elephant appears — memorise the eye position
  3. The elephant hides — point your index finger to where the eye was
  4. Pinch your fingers to drop the eye
  5. See how close you were!
